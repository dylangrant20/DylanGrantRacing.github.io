About Dylan

Dylan Grant (b. 20 Jan 2005, Auckland) started karting at Mt Wellington Kart Club aged six. Progressing through SpeedSport scholarship support into Formula First and then Formula Ford, Dylan captured the 2022/23 Giltrap Group North Island Formula Ford Series title along with Rookie of the Year and Class 1 honours.

With over 200 career race starts, Dylan is known for technical feedback, tyre management, and calm racecraft under pressure.
