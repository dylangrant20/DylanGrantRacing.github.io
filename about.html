About Dylan Grant

Dylan Grant (born 20 January 2005) is a New Zealand racing driver from Auckland. He began karting aged six at Mt Wellington Kart Club and progressed through the SpeedSport scholarship into Formula First and then Formula Ford. Known for a determined and technical approach, Dylan has enjoyed multiple wins and championship success in New Zealand Formula Ford.

Background

Dylan's early karting experience, combined with mentorship and scholarship support, helped launch a strong junior single-seater career. He is focused on refining racecraft, tyre management, and consistent results to climb the motorsport ladder.
